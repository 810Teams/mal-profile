　　<title>My Anime Scoring System</title>
　　　🏆 [b][[10]] Masterpiece[/b] - A true masterpiece, outstanding and perfectly composed, leaving a strong impression.
　　　🥇 [b][[9]] Ideal[/b] - An excellent anime, surprisingly well composed, leaving an impression.
　　　🥈 [b][[8]] Superior[/b] - A great anime, solid and well-rounded, giving a great experience.
　　　🥉 [b][[7]] Standard[/b] - A good anime, having everything an anime should have, enjoyable.
　　　🙆‍♂️ [b][[6]] Ordinary[/b] - A decent anime, containing some weak elements, giving a decent joy.
　　　🤷‍♂️ [b][[5]] Acceptable[/b] - A passable anime, missing some elements, giving a minimal amount of joy.
　　　🙅‍♂️ [b][[4]] Failure[/b] - A failed anime, containing an obvious issue, disappointing and unenjoyable.
　　　🤦‍♂️ [b][[3]] Bad[/b] - A bad anime, containing a serious issue, giving a displeasing feeling.
　　　💀 [b][[2]] Problematic[/b] - An anime itself is the issue, giving a difficult experience.
　　　💩 [b][[1]] Trash[/b] - Something horrifying, insulting, or offensively bad, which I want to unsee.
