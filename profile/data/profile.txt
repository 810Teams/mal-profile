　　<title>My Scoring System</title>
　　　🏆 [b][[10]] Masterpiece[/b] - A true masterpiece, outstanding and perfectly composed, leaving a strong impression.
　　　🥇 [b][[9]] Excellent[/b] - An ideal anime, undeniably great and surprisingly well composed, leaving an impression.
　　　🥈 [b][[8]] Great[/b] - A great anime, giving a solid and well-rounded experience, very enjoyable to me.
　　　🥉 [b][[7]] Good[/b] - A standard anime, having everything an anime should have, enjoyable to me.
　　　🙆‍♂️ [b][[6]] Fine[/b] - An ordinary anime, having some notable good but not strong points, giving a decent joy.
　　　🤷‍♂️ [b][[5]] Passable[/b] - An acceptable anime, being neither good nor bad, giving a minimal amount of joy.
　　　🙅‍♂️ [b][[4]] Poor[/b] - A failed anime, lacking something important or containing an obvious issue, disappointing to me.
　　　🤦‍♂️ [b][[3]] Bad[/b] - A bad anime, containing a serious issue, giving a displeasing or uncomfortable feelings.
　　　💀 [b][[2]] Problematic[/b] - An anime itself is the issue, sensing no effort in production, giving a difficult experience.
　　　💩 [b][[1]] Horrifying[/b] - Something horrifying, insulting, or offensively bad, which i resent and want to unsee.
